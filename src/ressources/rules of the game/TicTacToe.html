Rules of Tic-Tac-Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe is a simple game played between two players. Here are the rules:

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • One player uses the symbol "X" and the other uses "O".
  • Players take turns placing their symbol in an empty square.
  • The goal is to place three of your symbols in a row, column, or diagonal.
  • The first player to achieve this goal wins the game.
  • If all squares are filled without any player aligning three symbols, the game is declared a draw.

Have fun and may the best player win!
